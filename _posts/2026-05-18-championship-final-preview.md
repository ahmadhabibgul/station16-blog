The city has never felt more electric.

After years of bitter rivalry in rain-soaked lower-division grounds, City United and Riverside FC will finally meet on the biggest stage available to them — the National Cup Final.

Both managers remained tight-lipped at Friday's press conference, but the weight of the occasion was unmistakable. 'We've waited a long time for this,' United captain Marcus Delvecchio said. 'We're ready.'
