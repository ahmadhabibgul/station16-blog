Centre Court has witnessed upsets before, but seldom one as complete as this.

Mia Tanaka, seventeen years old and ranked 214th in the world, dismantled the defending champion in two hours and nine minutes of near-flawless grass-court tennis on Thursday afternoon.

When the final ball clipped the baseline and was called out, the teenager covered her face with both hands. Then she looked up at a crowd that was already on its feet, and burst into tears.
