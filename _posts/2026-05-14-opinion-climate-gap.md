The IPCC's latest synthesis report runs to nearly three thousand pages. Most people will not read it — that is understandable.

But here is what those pages say, stripped of scientific caveats: we are not on track. The gap between what countries have pledged and what they have actually delivered has widened every single year since the Paris Agreement was signed.

This is not a failure of knowledge. Everyone knows what must be done. It is a failure of political will, and that is a solvable problem — if we choose to solve it.
